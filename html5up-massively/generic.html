Brief Intro of me

My favorite animal is Unicorn. Some people do not believe there is Unicorn in the world, but I do.

I always dream of being an unicorn in Data Science field.

I am a super fan of data science. I not only enjoy revealing the hidden insights from data, but also care about how to apply those insights to business practices through product design and modeling R&D. My great satisfaction is derived from delivering modeling results and creating action plans.

With a bachelor’s degree in Finance, I am pursuing a master’s degree in Business Analytics at Bentley University currently, and working as a Data Scientist Intern at Plymouth Rock Assurance, one of the leading autos and home insurers in the Northeastern area. I had gained 3-years professional experience in data science, financial analytics and marketing analytics roles across various industries, and is recognized as a collaborative team player with excellent problem-solving strengths.

In my spare time, I enjoy learning advanced data science skills by absorbing new idea at Medium blog, and gaining certificates at online learning websites like Udemy, Coursera and Udacity.
